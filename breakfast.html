Breakfast Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Eggs 2
Milk 1/2 cup
Bread 2 slices

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs and mix in the milk.
  2. Dip the bread slices in the mixture.
  3. Cook on a hot skillet until golden brown.
  4. Serve warm with syrup or jam.

Final Dish
