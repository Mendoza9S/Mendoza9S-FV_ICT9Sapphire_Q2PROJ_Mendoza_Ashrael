Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Pasta 300g
Tomato Sauce 1 cup
Cheese 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Heat tomato sauce in a pan.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce and sprinkle cheese on top.
  4. Serve hot.
